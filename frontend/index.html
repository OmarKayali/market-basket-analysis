Key Terms & Metrics

Support

The frequency of an itemset in the dataset.

Support(X) = (Transactions containing X) / (Total transactions)

Confidence

The likelihood that item Y is purchased when item X is purchased.

Confidence(X → Y) = Support(X,Y) / Support(X)

Lift

Measures how much more likely items are bought together. Lift > 1 indicates positive correlation.

Lift(X → Y) = Confidence(X → Y) / Support(Y)

DBSCAN Clustering

Groups customers with similar purchase patterns. Noise (-1) represents outliers.
